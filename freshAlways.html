fresh Always

Description

This is a clone of disney app built with ReactJs., Firebase, React-Router-Dom

Getting fresh fruits and food delivered to your doorstep is a huge task, and everyone wants something good. FreshAlways is built using REACTJS, Firebase Authentication, FireStore , Firebase Storage, tailwind CSS and Framer motion to handle the animation. FreshAlways is a platform that makes it easy for customers to get access to freshly produced foods with a tap at their doorstep. With a click on their phone, the products are delivered to anywhere in their city. Users can login using their Google accounts.
